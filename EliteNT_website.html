Applications

Batteries

Batteries are now oppurtunity space for the use of our pulp and sheets. Similar materials are being integrated as dopants within upcoming Li-ion battery technologies. We now offer CNT sheets and pulp for multiple substitutable battery components.

  • Stabilizes Silicon Anodes improving performance by x 10.
  • Improves Charge/Discharge Rates.
  • Reduces thermal runaway risk through superior heat dissipation.
  • Increases battery efficiency and drive range through weight reduction.

Disperions

We offer organic and water-based CNT dispersions as an additive to your market needs. These have critical applications ranging from improving corrosion resistance in mairitime components to thin-film paints for electrical conductive sheets. They integrate well in polymers and can be used to enhance electrical conductivity.

  • Enhance electrical discharge in polymers.
  • Improves corrosion resistance whilst enhancing conductivity of the paints in maritime applications.
  • Provides a netowrk for thin-film applications.
  • Enhances thermal conductivity of ceramics and other possible high-temperature applications.

Aerospace Composites

The quality of our CNTs is essential for lightweight composite structures and EMI shielding in high-altitude/vacuum environments, where material integrity is of utmost importance.

  • Eliminates particulate shedding/contamination risk across several critial components.
  • 9x weight reduction over traditional copper based products.
  • Superior mechanical strength and thermal transfer in critical airframes.
  • Operate in high stress environments in extreme radiation and vacuums.
