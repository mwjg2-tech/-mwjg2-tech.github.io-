Mission-Critical Applications

Automotive / EV Energy Storage

Our EliteNT_1 enhances Li-ion battery performance by creating a highly efficient conductive network. This stabilizes the silicon anode during volume expansion, directly increasing energy-density and cycle life.

  • Stabilizes Silicon Anodes improving performance by x 10.
  • Improves Charge/Discharge Rates.
  • Reduces thermal runaway risk through superior heat dissipation.

Aerospace Composites

The non-flaking nature of our CNTs is essential for lightweight composite structures and EMI shielding in high-altitude/vacuum environments, where material integrity is of utmost importance.

  • Eliminates particulate shedding/contamination risk.
  • 9x weight reduction over traditional copper based products.
  • Superior mechanical strength and thermal transfer in critical airframes.
